About

Project Background:

This project, "A Deep Learning and Cloud-Based IoT Integrated System For Decoding Speech Imagery EEG Signals," centers on the utilization of pre-collected EEG (Electroencephalogram) data, obtained from EEG devices to decode four specific imagined words: "up," "down," and "left," and "right." This project employs deep learning to enable individuals to control computer interfaces with their cognitive processes, advancing assistive technology and refining human-computer interaction

Proposed Solution:

Our product integrates deep learning with cloudbased IoT and EEG signal processing technologies. It interprets EEG signals to decode imagery words (right, left, up, down) via Brain-Computer Interface (BCI) for improved communication.
